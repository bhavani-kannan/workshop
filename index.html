IBM Consulting | Manufacturing Decision Intelligence

Agentic AI Readiness for Manufacturing

A structured five-day engagement to surface the decisions that drive plant performance, validate data readiness, define decision logic, and arrive at a build-ready use case.

Each day produces one core operational intelligence object. Together, these five objects capture operational reality, decision intelligence, signal readiness, governance boundaries, and validation evidence. They can be fed into Copilot or another LLM to generate the final Operational Playbook and implementation blueprint. The BA's role is not to produce every document manually. It is to extract the human judgment, tacit knowledge, trust dynamics, and operational deviations that no AI can reliably infer from a transcript alone.

Pre-Workshop Preparation

The quality of the five-day workshop is determined before Day 1 begins. This phase defines what the client provides, what each stakeholder prepares, what IBM pre-populates, and the readiness gate that must be passed before the workshop proceeds.
